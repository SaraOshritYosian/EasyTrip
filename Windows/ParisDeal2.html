Hôtel Des Arts-Bastille

Hotel des Arts-Bastille is located in Paris, in the heart of the Charonne district, a 15-minute walk from the trendy Marais District and Bastille. It offers stylish, modern, soundproofed rooms decorated by local artists and free high-speed Wi-Fi access. Each room is equipped with a TV, wooden flooring, a seating area and a private bathroom. All rooms are accessible by a lift.
