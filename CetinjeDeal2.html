Kalamper Hotel & Spa

Set in Dobra Voda in the Bar County Region, 100 metres from Veliki Pijesak Beach, Kalamper Hotel & Spa boasts a spa centre and a private beach area. Guests can enjoy the on-site restaurant. Kalamper Hotel & Spa features free WiFi throughout the property. All rooms have a TV with satellite channels. Some units also include a seating area to relax in after a busy day. All rooms include a private bathroom fitted with a bath or shower. For your comfort, you will find bathrobes and slippers. You will find a 24-hour front desk at the property and video-surveilled parking. This hotel has water sports facilities and bike hire is available.
