Raeli Hotel Archimede

Only 150 metres from Termini Train Station, Hotel Archimede offers a free Wi-Fi zone and a comfortable lounge area. Its air-conditioned rooms have a classic design and all feature an LCD TV. Each room has a minibar and a private bathroom with a hairdryer and toiletries. The Archimede Hotel is a 19th-century building that has been completely renovated.
