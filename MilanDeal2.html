The Corner Duomo Hotel

Just a 3-minute walk from the Duomo, the Corner Duomo Hotel is located in the center of Milan, a few steps from the Teatro alla Scala and Via Monte Napoleone. The hotel includes private spa with Jacuzzi, sauna , Turkish bath, emotional shower and fitness room. The rooms and suites are air-conditioned and equipped with flat-screen satellite smart TVs, Nespresso coffee station, free Wi-Fi..
